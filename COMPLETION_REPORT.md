## 🎨 Quick Customization

### 1. Change Clinic Name (5 minutes)
Find & Replace: "Star Dental" → "Your Clinic"
- Navigation.jsx
- Footer.jsx
- Home.jsx
- Contact.jsx

### 2. Update Contact Info (5 minutes)
Edit in:
- src/components/Footer.jsx
- src/pages/Contact.jsx
Phone, email, address, hours

### 3. Modify Colors (2 minutes)
Edit `tailwind.config.js`:
```javascript
primary: '#0EA5E9'    → Your color
secondary: '#10B981'  → Your color
accent: '#F0FDFA'     → Your color
```

### 4. Replace Images (10 minutes)
Replace emoji placeholders with real images
- src/pages/Home.jsx (line 80+)
- src/pages/About.jsx (line 30+)
- src/components/DoctorCard.jsx
- etc.
